Plot

Empuraan is the highly anticipated sequel to the Malayalam blockbuster "Lucifer," directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran and written by Murali Gopy. The film explores the mysterious backstory of Stephen Nedumpally, who is revealed to be Khureshi Ab’ram, a powerful global figure operating under the radar. "Empuraan" digs deeper into political conspiracies, global crime networks, and the making of a dark empire.

Characters

Stephen Nedumpally, aka Khureshi Ab’ram, is the central character whose double life unravels in this sequel. Powerful, strategic, and enigmatic, he controls an empire that spans continents. Supporting characters include politicians, police officials, and adversaries from Lucifer's world.

Reviews & Buzz

Empuraan has generated massive anticipation among fans and critics alike. The teaser and announcement glimpses received millions of views within hours, with praise for Mohanlal's intense portrayal and Prithviraj's cinematic vision.
