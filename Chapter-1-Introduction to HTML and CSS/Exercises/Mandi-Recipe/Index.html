Mandi

Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish made with basmati rice, tender meat, and fragrant spices. It’s slow-cooked and full of deep flavor – perfect for special occasions or a comforting meal at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut in halves)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Saffron in warm milk (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add garlic, tomato, and spices. Cook until soft.
  4. Add rice and stir well.
  5. Pour in broth and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Place chicken on top, cover with foil, and bake for 35 minutes.
  7. Drizzle with saffron milk before serving (optional).
  8. Enjoy with salad or yogurt.
